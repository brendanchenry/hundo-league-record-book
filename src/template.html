            About the data. Compiled from 17 completed seasons (2009–2025), 198 manager-seasons. The league ran 10 teams through 2011 and 12 teams since 2012. A few honest gaps: four 2009 teams are recorded without a manager name and appear as Unknown; final placements are on record only for 1st through 6th, so missed-playoff seasons are shown as unranked; and trades are logged as a per-season count, not as head-to-head deals. Numbers are exactly as they appear in the league archive.
